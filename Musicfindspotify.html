Welcome to our Spotify Page!

Spotify is a music application made by Spotify Ltd it is free to download on computers, smartphones and gaming consoles. Spotify is the #1 music app on the apple app store. With Spotify you can custom make your own playlist or listen to other people's playlist. Spotify will also suggest music for you and alert you when a artist you follow uploads a new song.If you choose to use spotify for free you will get ads and you can only shuffle play playlists on mobile. If you pay $10 a month for Spotify Premium you dont get ads and can listen to specfic songs on demand using Spotify.

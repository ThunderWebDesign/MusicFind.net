Welcome to our Youtube Music page!

YouTube music is The #4 music app on the Apple app store owned by Alphabet inc. also known as Google. Like Spotify YouTube music allows the user to listen for free with ads. To avoid ads you must pay for a premium service which cost $13 a month. With YouTube Music you can listen to playlist or listen to specific songs on demand. Youtube music will allow you to search up songs by lyrics and will recommend songs based on past music you have listened to.
